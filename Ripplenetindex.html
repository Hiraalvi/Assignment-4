RIPPLE NET

RippleNet is Ripple's global payment network that is used by more than two hundred banks and payment providers worldwide today. Using RippleNet, customers can transfer funds to any other user on the network in seconds at a low fee and high visibility and transparency. Ripple offers three products for international money transfer: xVia, xCurrent and xRaipd.

RippleNet provide a single platform to quickly introduce new corridors, offer lower fees and faster transaction times - all while reducing operational overhead. Our customers now have low-cost traceable payment services in and out of more than 55 countries worldwide.

RippleNet is a Ripple product that is build on XRP ledger and used as a payment and exchange network. You have two choices to join RippleNet network. xVia xCurrent



xVia (STANDARD ACCESS)

xVia is the standard payment interface for joining the RippleNet network, and it comes with all the default features of ripple we just discussed. With xVia, one does not need to install any additional software and banks can start trying xVia as the first step for sending money globally.

xCurrent (FULL ACCESS)

xCurrent is the enterprises international remittance solution from Ripple, and it is their most popular product in the market, used by many major banks in the world. Using xCurrent, banks can transfer funds to different currencies as per the current forex rate. It provides a single source of truth for the transacting counterparties while preservaing tge privacy of banking customers' identifiable payment information.
